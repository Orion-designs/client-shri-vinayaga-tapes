Shri Vinayaga Tapes — Website
A modern, responsive business website for Shri Vinayaga Tapes, a manufacturer of high-quality adhesive tapes. The site showcases the company's product range, brand story, and provides an easy way for customers to get in touch.
Live site: https://shrivinayaga-tapes.netlify.app/
About the project
This website was built to give Shri Vinayaga Tapes a professional online presence, highlighting their manufacturing expertise, product catalog, and customer trust built over a decade in the industry.
Sections
Home — Landing section introducing the brand.
About — Company background, years of experience, manufacturing capabilities, key stats (years of experience, happy clients, products), brand story, and a "Why Choose Us" feature breakdown.
Products — An interactive carousel showcasing the full tape range, including plain, brown, jumbo, custom, and colour-coded tapes (red, blue, green, yellow, orange, white, black, purple, and fully custom colours).
Contact — A contact form with product selection dropdown, direct email and phone details, social media links, and a floating WhatsApp chat button for instant inquiries.
Footer — Quick navigation, contact details, and copyright information.
Features

Fully responsive design across desktop, tablet, and mobile
Smooth scroll-reveal animations as sections come into view
Interactive product carousel with left/right navigation
Custom dropdown component for product selection in the contact form
Direct WhatsApp integration for quick customer outreach
Clean section transitions using wave dividers and alternating backgrounds

Built with

HTML
CSS (custom styling, no framework)
JavaScript
EmailJS (for contact form submissions)
